Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Next Caltrain ("the app") is designed to work without collecting any personal information about you. This page explains exactly what data the app uses and how.

Information We Collect

We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app has no account or sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking software of any kind.

Network Requests

The app fetches a schedule data file (schedule.json) from next-caltrain-pwa.appspot.com when it launches, and periodically afterward to keep train times up to date. This request does not include any information that identifies you or your device — it's the same request made by every user of the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your selected stations (for example, your usual morning and evening stops) are saved locally on your device using Apple's standard preferences storage (UserDefaults). A cached copy of the train schedule is also stored locally so the app can show information quickly on future launches. None of this data ever leaves your device, and we have no way to access it.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, because it does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@netpress.com.
